Professional Summary

I am an experienced educator, leader, manager, engineer, and author with 30 years of progressive accomplishment across government, defense, commercial, and academic organizations. I have built, operated, and modernized complex enterprise systems while leading high-performing technical teams and mentoring the next generation of computing professionals.

Today, my work blends full-time computer science instruction at Hillsborough Community College, long-standing adjunct faculty work with UMGC, and consulting through my company for federal and defense pursuits, cybersecurity programs, and technology strategy. In parallel, I serve in senior IEEE leadership and regional volunteer roles that expand professional development pathways for members and students.

Current focus: Computer science instruction, cybersecurity and proposal consulting, and IEEE leadership in membership and geographic activities.
